About Our College

Welcome to the College Bulletin - your premier source for information about our prestigious MBA and PGDM programs. Established in 1985, we have been at the forefront of business education, developing leaders who shape the future of industry and commerce.

Our campus spans 50 acres with state-of-the-art facilities including modern classrooms, a comprehensive business library, innovation labs, and collaborative learning spaces. We pride ourselves on our diverse community of over 3,000 students and 200 faculty members.
